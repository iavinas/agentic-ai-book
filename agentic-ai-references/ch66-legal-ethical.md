# Chapter 66: Legal, Ethical, and Societal Implications

## Core Thesis
EU AI Act (no legal definition of "agent"), US patchwork, UK sectoral, China security-first. Liability, labor displacement, and autonomous weapons are the core societal issues.

## Key References

### Regulatory Landscape
- **EU AI Act** — Agents fall under GPAI model + system layer obligations. No legal definition of "AI agent". Runtime behavioral drift may constitute "substantial modification". Multi-party transparency required. Standards: CEN/CENELEC JTC 21 (target Q4 2026).
- **United States** — NIST AI Agent Standards Initiative (Feb 2026). FTC enforcement under Section 5 (Mar 2026). State fragmentation: Colorado, California, Texas.
- **United Kingdom** — CMA: businesses legally responsible for AI agent consumer law violations (Mar 2026). ICO: AI and biometrics strategy update (Mar 2026).
- **China** — Comprehensive, security-first. Filing obligations, cross-border data transfer rules, 4-hour incident reporting.
